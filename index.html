ABOUT UNLIMITED

Unlimited is the passion project of Isabel Nixon. Frustrated with the attention economy of today, she set out to display the finest - and most annoying - parts of the Information Era. Explore the site. Laugh at its gaudy stupidity and the unironic use of Comic Sans as a design element. Remember what was good and use it to take a break from being marketed to and influenced.
