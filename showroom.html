Place your phone across the room at night and wake up slowly with natural light.

Field Notes are cheap, non-daunting notebooks that act as a catch-all for your daily lists, thoughts, and plans. Any notebook will do, but if you're new to organizing everything on paper, these are a great start at only 48 pages.

Evora Watches are customizable with multiple modes and pieces of information all in one watch face. It's a perfect blend of vital information at a glance to keep you from pulling out your phone to check the time or date and getting distracted by notifications.

Libraries are one of the best resources for anything you want to know. Instead of a Google deep dive, why not skim a book from the library? And if you need something to fill your time after digital decluttering, libraries are the way to go.

> Websites
DUMBPHONE FINDER STAYFOCUSD NEWSMINIMALIST r/DIGITALMINIMALISM

Created by Jose Briones, the Dumbphone Finder is a great resource for anyone looking for a non-smartphone to fulfill their phone-related needs. Filter by any apps you are set on keeping and any features that are important to you and Dumbphone Finder will find the perfect companion for you.

StayFocusd is the ultimate Chrome extension for blocking distracting websites. Choose which sites you want to block, or choose the few you want to allow, and schedule your site-blocking time depending on how many hours and days you need more focus. The extension requires you to complete a challenge (i.e. typing a 500 word paragraph from prompt) to access blocked sites, giving you a minute to consider your intention before breaking your focus.

NewsMinimalist uses ChatGPT to read the top stories of the day and rates them with a "significance score" depending on magnitude, scale, and novelty. This weighted score is then used to compile the most significant stories so you can filter through the noise of news media.

My deepest apologies for adding a Reddit page on this site, BUT an important part of making digital minimalism work is having a support system of people learning with you.
